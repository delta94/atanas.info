Hi!

My name is Atanas and I am a JavaScript Developer.

I am currently located in Varna, Bulgaria.

Many friends refer to me as "uncle" because I was born in the previous millennium but I am actually not that old.
Being born in the 1980s has its pros and cons.

The main pro about it is the fact that I've had enough time to decide what matters to me.

I love coding!

I started doing this in 2010 as an apprentice to a hardcore freelancer and pretty fast learned the basics of HTML and CSS.

Between 2012 and 2018 i was full-time employed in one of the best companies in my country - 2create

Since the beginnign of 2018 i am employed in a start-up company located in Varna and operating internationally.

I've grown up.

I started as a front-end developer and my daily tasks included HTML and CSS coding.

As months went by, i started using pre- and post-processors in CSS and slowly mastered LESS, SASS and POSTCSS which opened the way for learning how to use task runners such as Grunt, Gulp and Webpack.

Along CSS i learned JavaScript and it became my preferred language.
I love it and it appears it shares the feelings.

The best day ever

In the late 2015 a magical thing happened that transformed my entire life and gave it a new purpose:
My beloved wife gave birth to our first child - Stefan.
Our lives have new meaning now.
This is the best thing that can happen to a man!

Today

Now i am a full-stack web developer focused on the front-end but working with back-end technologies as well.
My daily tasks include coding JS on the front and on the back as well as advanced CSS and PHP.
